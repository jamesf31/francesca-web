<b>Digital Not-Spot, 2024</b>



An abstraction of the idea of a perfect circular ‘spot’ pattern, which used shape sourced from vegetable cross sections creating a ‘not-spot’. Process included clamp dying potato slices, creating vegetable stacks, and block printing using veg. A collection of vivid paperwork created the basis for a digital print collection. The process was greatly enjoyable because I love vegetables.
