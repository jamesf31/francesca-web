<b>Biscuit Heads, 2025 </b>



Digestive biscuit heads, pre and post baking. Made from the remnants of a friend’s dissertation project which enquired how long different brands of digestives took to break in a cup of tea. The soggy digestives were a goldmine of sculptural material that looked like kinetic sand. So hilariously ugly.
